What Makes a Movie Successful?

The film industry is one of the most influential and profitable entertainment sectors in the world. This project dives into a rich dataset spanning over decades (1960 - 2025) of cinematic history.

Explore how financial success, critical acclaim, and industry recognition each tell a different story.

Financial Success

Action films deliver over 140% ROI on average, outpacing drama and comedy.

Critical Acclaim

Critics’ scores only explain about 30% of box-office variation—audiences march to their own beat.

Industry Recognition

Only 5% of films win major awards—but those boost gross by over 40%.
